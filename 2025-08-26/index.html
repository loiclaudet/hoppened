Urbanist

The Urbanist font, created by designer Corey Hu, was launched in 2020 as a low-contrast, geometric sans-serif typeface inspired by Modernist design.

Conceived from elementary geometric shapes, it was designed for neutrality and versatility across digital and print media.

Initially released with 9 weights and italics, it has since evolved into a flexible variable font, making it a popular, utilitarian choice for various applications.
